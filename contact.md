## Before You Contact Us

### Check the FAQ

Many common questions are already answered in our [FAQ](/faq/). You might find what you're looking for there.

### Technical Support

We're happy to help with general de-Googling questions, but for technical support with Proton products, please contact [Proton Support](https://proton.me/support) directly. They have dedicated support staff who can help with account-specific issues.

### Response Time

We're a small team passionate about privacy, not a large corporation with 24/7 support. We do our best to respond within a few business days, but please be patient during busy periods.
